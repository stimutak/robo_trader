## Success Metrics

### Technical Targets
- **Uptime**: 99.9% system availability
- **Latency**: <100ms trading decision to order submission  
- **Throughput**: Support 200+ symbols concurrently
- **Reliability**: <0.1% error rate across all operations

### Business Targets  
- **Risk-Adjusted Returns**: Sharpe ratio >1.5
- **Drawdown Control**: Maximum drawdown <10%
- **Risk Management**: All risk limits enforced with <100ms latency
- **Operational Excellence**: Zero manual interventions during trading hours

This plan builds upon the existing Phase 3 completion (S1-S4 done) and focuses on production hardening, reliability, and operational excellence. The staged approach ensures systematic improvement while maintaining the current trading operations.
